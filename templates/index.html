How It Works

User Registration

Users are registered with their facial data through a simple enrollment process. Multiple images are captured to ensure accurate recognition.

Attendance Marking

The system uses real-time face detection to identify users. When a user's face is recognized, their attendance is automatically recorded with timestamp.

Data Management

Attendance records are stored securely and can be viewed, filtered, and exported. The system maintains detailed logs of all attendance activities.
